# IG Ad Copy — "The Conversation Before The Listing"

Campaign objective: surface as a familiar name to coastal SoCal homeowners thinking about selling in the next 1–2 years, route the curious to a no-obligation home value report.

Brand voice rules in effect:
- No em-dashes (commas, periods, short splits only)
- No "clients" / "your buyers" language (the reader is the client)
- No demand overpromise ("I have buyers looking" is banned)
- No street-level locators (city is the right specificity)
- Meet the reader where they are; position the home for the best result

Meta caveat: under Special Ad Category Housing we cannot age/gender/ZIP-target or use exclusions. Copy needs to land cleanly with a broad coastal-OC audience.

## Capo Beach (Dana Point)

**Primary text variants (Meta Feed, ~125 chars preview, full text 2,200 max)**

1. The best Capo Beach listings start a year or two before they hit the market. If you're thinking about selling, let's start the conversation now and get your home positioned to bring the best result when the timing is right.

2. Most homeowners wait until they're ready to list to call an agent. The result is usually a rushed sale. The early conversation is where the real positioning happens, so when your Capo Beach home goes to market, it goes out at its strongest.

3. You don't have to be ready to sell to talk about selling. A real read on your Capo Beach home, the comps that matter, and the strategy that gets you the best result. Sent over by me, no pressure to list.
