# Postcard Series: "Quietly Looking"

A three-card neighborhood-targeted seller-prospecting series. Built as a sister piece to `StateOfNeigborhood_Mailer_9x6_0413_Print.pdf`, sharing the same 6x9 landscape layout, typography system, and Sotheby's lockup placement. Each card carries a distinct call-to-action: **I have qualified buyers searching specifically in your neighborhood. Are you open to selling?**

The QR code on each card routes to the home-value (free CMA) form on adamboehrer.com, tagged with neighborhood-specific UTMs for tracking.

### Brand Rules (Hard Constraints)

- Colors only: SIR Blue `#002349`, Gold `#C29B40`, Text Grey `#666666`, White `#FFFFFF`. Background: white only.
- Typography: serif (Freight / Mercury) for display, sans-serif (Benton Sans) for body. No weight heavier than semibold. Never underline.
- Gold reserved for the two thin horizontal rules. No gold backgrounds.
- Headshot: 3:2, color, no filter, no overlay text.
- No em-dashes anywhere in body copy.
- No reference to specific street names. "San Clemente," "Capo Beach," "Broadmoor," "Hillcrest" are the geographic specificity ceiling.
- No "client" framing toward the recipient. They are not an agent with their own buyers; they are the homeowner.

## Production Notes

1. **QR generation.** Generate three QRs from the three URLs above. Test scan each before sending to print. The destination loads the home-value form on adamboehrer.com with the campaign tagged in the URL.
2. **Tracking.** UTMs land in any analytics on the site. If a recipient submits the home-value form, the campaign tag will be attached to that lead, so you can attribute by neighborhood.
3. **Print spec.** Match the existing card: 6x9 landscape, full bleed where the existing card uses bleed, four-color process. Bring this brief and the existing PDF to the same designer for visual consistency.
4. **Mail list.** Pair Card 1 with the Capo Beach polygon owner list, Card 2 with the Broadmoor list, Card 3 with the Hillcrest list. Suppress any owners who have already replied to the previous mailer or who are currently listed with another broker.
5. **Voice integrity check before going to print.** No em-dashes. No "clients." No street names. Eyebrow neighborhood label spelled correctly on each card.
