## What this is

A series of three single-sided 9 × 6 inch postcards, one per neighborhood, mailing to homeowners in Capo Beach (Dana Point), Broadmoor (San Clemente), and Hillcrest (San Clemente). Same headline and CTA across all three; the eyebrow personalization and lifestyle subhead change per neighborhood.

The angle is buyer-demand-led seller prospecting. The reader sees confirmation of what they already love about their neighborhood, learns there are buyers actively looking for it, and can either scan the QR for a no-obligation home value report or reach out to me directly.

## Notes on intent

- **Lifestyle line is the social proof.** It mirrors back what the homeowner already loves about the neighborhood, which validates the "I have buyers looking" hook above. Reads as "you have something special, and other people want it."
- **QR + marker annotation works as a soft CTA** for readers who don't want to call. Hand-drawn arrow points at the QR; gold marker font matches the website style.
- **Headshot grounds the CTA personally** — readers see a face, not just a logo. Same headshot used on adamboehrer.com and the Coastal Currents newsletter, so it's recognizable across touchpoints.
