# LinkedIn Daily Ideas Backlog

Three finished, copy-paste-ready drafts per day, generated by the `linkedin-ideas` skill
(run `/linkedin-ideas`). This is the deep bench. Pull the strongest drafts from here whenever
you want to post. Nothing here is published automatically.

After you post one, change its status to `POSTED` (or move it to `posted/`). Voice rules and
pillar definitions live in `PILLARS.md`. No hashtags on LinkedIn.
