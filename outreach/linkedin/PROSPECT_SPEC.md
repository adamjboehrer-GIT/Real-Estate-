# LinkedIn Prospecting Spec — Adam Boehrer

**Goal:** Identify and connect with high-income, community-invested professionals and business owners in coastal South OC / North SD County. Build genuine local relationships, top-of-mind real estate presence. Precision over volume.

**Account tier:** FREE LinkedIn (commercial-use search limit applies — search sparingly, human-paced). No Sales Navigator.

## Target geography (priority order)
1. San Clemente
2. Dana Point (incl. Capistrano Beach / Capo Beach — Adam's home turf)
3. Laguna Beach
4. Newport Beach
5. Oceanside
6. Carlsbad

## Who we're after
Established professionals and **business owners** who are clearly **invested in their community** — not transient, not junior. The "most invested in the community" filter is the heart of this.

**Roles / categories:**
- Business owners (founders, owners, principals — local businesses especially)
- Doctors / physicians / dentists / surgeons
- Financial advisors / wealth managers / CPAs
- Attorneys / lawyers (partners, principals)
- Tech (founders, execs, senior leaders)
- Open-ended: "you name it" — any senior/owner-level professional rooted locally

**Community-investment signals to prioritize:**
- Owns/runs a business physically located in a target city
- Long tenure in the area, local volunteer/board/chamber involvement
- Local references in headline/about (kids' schools, local orgs, "proudly serving [city]")

## Operating rules (account safety — FREE account, LinkedIn is strict)
- Low volume, human-paced. No bursts. Treat invites as a slow drip.
- Personalized connection notes only — never blank/templated blasts.
- Everything drafted for Adam's approval before sending. Adam often clicks send.
- Stop immediately on any "you've reached the limit" / commercial-use / checkpoint warning; log and report.
- Income is never a LinkedIn field — it's always inferred from title + seniority + industry + geography.

## Tracking
All targets and touches logged to `database/leads.db` → `linkedin_prospects` table.
connect_status flow: identified → queued → note_drafted → request_sent → connected → replied

## Scope (per Adam, 2026-06-03)
- On-demand only (no auto-schedule).
- Engagement focus: connection requests + inbound DM triage. NOT proactive commenting / not reply-to-post monitoring.
