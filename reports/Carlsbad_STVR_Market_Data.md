## Key Takeaways

1. **This property does not cash flow at $3,150,000.** Even at strong STR performance, Rahm and Tunisia carry ~$3,900–$6,900/month out of pocket.

2. **STR rates in Terramar are strong** — peak summer comps show $842–$2,128/night for comparable 3BR properties. This is a premium STR market.

3. **The on-site manager arrangement is valuable.** Eliminates ~$1,700–$2,100/month in property management costs compared to a typical absentee-owner STR operation.

4. **This is an appreciation play, not a cash flow investment.** Terramar is a tightly held, high-demand coastal market. The bet is on long-term equity growth while subsidizing the carry.

5. **Breakeven purchase price** (at realistic scenario): approximately **$1,800,000–$2,000,000** — where STR + ADU income would cover PITI at 6%.
